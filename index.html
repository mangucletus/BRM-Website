BRM Program

....helping deprived but brilliant children, girls and women to unleash their potentials

Basic Resilience Mentorship (BRM) program is a non-profit organization that supports deprived but brilliant children, girls and women to unleash their potentials in the Savannah Region, Ghana

Vision

A society where everyone has equal opportunities to achieve their dreams

Mission

To build a community that liberate the less privileged from hardship through mentorship in educaction and skills training

Core Values

Leadership, SDG1 & SDG4 focused, Team Work, Community Transformation

Our Achievement so far

Creating awareness and educating students and children on Covid-19 pandemic, sharing nose mask and hand sanitizers

Tanigna D/A Primary & JHS

Involving parents to help mentor and nurture our mentees through house visitations and intensive guidance

Jentilpe, Sawla, Kakiasi

Donating makerbords and markers to enhance easier teaching and learning among teachers and students

Basic Schools

Educating students and our mentees on Adolescent Reproductive health, child Rights, time management and many more
